# Tilemap Metadata

## Tile Size: 8x8 pixels
## Image Size: 256x256 pixels (32x32 tiles)

## Sections:
- Row 0-1: Terrain (soil, grass, etc.)
- Row 2-3: Water (4 frames animation)
- Row 4-7: Residential (empty, making, low, middle, high)
- Row 8-11: Commercial (empty, making, low, middle, high)
- Row 12-15: Industrial (empty, making, low, middle, high)
- Row 16-17: Agricultural (fields, greenhouse, ranch)
- Row 18-19: Roads (all connection patterns)
- Row 20-23: Power facilities
- Row 24-27: Public facilities (hospital, police, fire, school)
- Row 28-31: Parks, forests, and effects

## Special tiles:
- 16x16 tiles: Middle density buildings, some facilities
- 32x32 tiles: High density buildings, large facilities

## Animation frames:
- Water: 4 frames (horizontal)
- Fire: 4 frames (horizontal)
- Construction: 4 frames (horizontal)
- Wind turbine: 3 frames (horizontal)
