# Simplified Controls Guide for ConcLand

## 🎮 Basic Controls

### Cursor Movement
- **Arrow Keys**: ⬆️⬇️⬅️➡️
- **Or K/J/H/L Keys** (Vim-style)

### Actions
- **Space Key** or **Z Key**: Place building
- **X Key**: Demolish building
- **H Key**: Toggle help display (always available!)

## 💡 Useful Features

### View Modes
- **V Key**: Cycle view modes
  - Normal → Pollution → Land Value → Power → Traffic → Normal...

### Detailed UI Panels
- **S**: Statistics Panel (population, employment, etc.)
- **E**: Economy Panel (resources, market, etc.)
- **T**: Traffic Panel (congestion, routes, etc.)
- **D**: Disaster Panel (disasters, emergency services)
- **P**: Policies Panel (taxes, policies)

### Tool Palette
- **B Key**: Show all tools palette
  - Left/Right arrows to select
  - Space to confirm
  - ESC to cancel

## 🚀 First Time Guide

### Step 1: Build Roads
1. Press **R Key** to select "Road"
2. Move cursor with **Arrow Keys**
3. Press **Space Key** to place road

### Step 2: Build Residential
1. Press **Q Key** to select "Residential"
2. Place with **Space Key** adjacent to road
3. People will start moving in

### Step 3: Supply Power
1. Press **I Key** to select "Power Plant"
2. Wait if funds are insufficient
3. Press **U Key** to select wires, connect from plant to residential

### Step 4: Watch Development
- Over time, residences will develop
- Population growth increases tax revenue
- Build more facilities as funds allow

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Insufficient Funds
- **Solution**: Wait for residential growth to increase tax revenue
- **Tip**: Focus on roads and residential areas first

### Population Not Growing
- **Check Points**:
  - Is it adjacent to a road?
  - Is power connected?
  - Is pollution too high?
- **Solution**: Place parks to increase land value

### Don't Remember Controls
- Press **H Key** anytime! The controls guide is always available
- Press **ESC Key** to close UI panels

## 🎓 Advanced Tips

### Efficient City Planning
1. Balance of **Residential → Commercial → Industrial** is important
2. Placing parks and police stations increases surrounding land value
3. Industry generates pollution, place far from residential

### Money Saving Tips
- Initially focus on roads and residential
- Establish power grid early
- Public services when funds allow

### Information Utilization
- **V Key** to check city status by switching views
- Less pollution = higher land value
- Traffic congestion prevents commercial development
